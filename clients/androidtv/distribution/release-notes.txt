KROMA Android TV 0.1.32

Android TV / Google TV / Fire TV / Nvidia Shield client for the self-hosted
KROMA media server. Remote-driven (D-pad) interface, native ExoPlayer playback
with a libVLC software fallback for codecs the device cannot decode.

What to test:
- Connecting to the server (Quick Connect from the mobile app, or manual entry)
- D-pad navigation through the movies / shows / genres library
- ExoPlayer playback (HEVC, 4K) and the libVLC fallback
- Audio tracks and subtitles
- The "Continue watching" row in the Android TV launcher

Install: sideload the APK. The Firebase App Tester app does not exist on
Android TV, see BETA.md for the steps.

Requires a KROMA server reachable from the TV's network.
Feedback: beta@kroma.tv
