KROMA Mobile 0.1.3 - first public beta

Mobile client for the self-hosted KROMA media server.

What to test:
- Connecting to your KROMA server (automatic discovery on the local network)
- Movies / shows / genres library, search
- Direct HEVC and 4K playback, automatic remux otherwise
- Multiple audio tracks, embedded and AI-generated subtitles
- Offline downloads (films and episodes, with their subtitles)
- Multiple servers and profiles, PIN lock
- Signing in a TV by scanning a QR code

Requires a KROMA server reachable from your phone.
Feedback: beta@kroma.tv
